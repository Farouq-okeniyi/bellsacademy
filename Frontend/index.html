Courses We Offer

Explore the top computer science courses offered by Bells University of Technology, tailored to provide students with the skills needed for a successful tech career.

BCA

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at Bells University of Technology is a three-year full-time course designed to equip students with a strong foundation in computer applications. It prepares graduates for advanced careers in software development, data analysis, and IT management.

BSc-IT

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSc-IT) program at Bells University focuses on managing, securing, and processing information. The curriculum emphasizes software engineering, database management, and computer networking, ensuring students are industry-ready.

BSc CA & IT

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Applications and Information Technology (BSc CA & IT) program is a three-year course that delves into the design, development, and application of computer systems. Bells University prepares students with cutting-edge knowledge for careers in tech innovation and system development.
